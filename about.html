Sylvia Altman creates art and chaos

Hi, I’m Sylvia. I have been making art my whole life. I love sewing, cooking, and drinking coffee. Right now I’m studying glass bowing at Cleveland Instatute of Art. I discovered my love for glass when I was eight and I stated working with glass when I was fourteen.

One of my biggest inspirations is nature. I love spending time outside exploring, but when im not doing that I’m taking care of my numerous house plants. As a craftsperson I am fascinated with the intersection of art and unable objects. I use my work as a way to remind people there is beauty in the mundane and that there is use in the artistic.
